What is Skully Inc.?

Skully Inc. is a 500 NFT collection of hand drawn 1/1 illustrations by self-taught 2D artist and animator, Kodak LDN.

This isn’t a generative PFP project. This is the result of the hours and months of dedication and work by a passionate artist. There is no official rarity ranking because each one is special.

If you’re into streetwear, anime or pop culture, there’s a Skully for you!

The Future

Shortly after reveal, Kodak will begin creating animations for every single Skully. The metadata will be updated and each holder will receive their unique animation.

In addition, Kodak plans to host an animation course to help interested individuals in Lagos, Nigeria and virtually for holders internationally.
Note From the Artist
Skully Inc. was inspired by my desire to be open and vulnerable as an artist. It is a metaphor of being able to see someone beyond their physical attributes.

I combined this idea with my love for fashion and pop culture and thus the collection was born. Skully Inc. was a way to challenge myself to create 500 unique illustrations, without help from any generative software.

It is also a reminder to web3 to look beyond the usual PFP projects and recognize there are talented 1/1 artists building in the space as well.
